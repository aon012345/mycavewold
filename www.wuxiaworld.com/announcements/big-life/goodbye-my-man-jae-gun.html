Goodbye my man, Jae-Gun!

Hi everyone! Big Life will soon be coming to an end.

The last chapter will be released to the public on 16th June 2024, and the end dates for the respective tiers are as follows: 

Rika: "Meow": 14th June 2024

Ancient Laptop: 12th June 2024

Magical Cup: 8th June 2024

Mysterious Fountain Pen: 4th June 2024

Horn-Rimmed Glasses: 29th May 2024

Literary Novels FTW: 23rd May 2024

Fantasy Novels FTW: 15th May 2024



Translator's & Editor's Last Words:

From J.Andie:

It's been a great journey sharing the story of Jae-Gun, his career, his friends and family with everyone! I wonder what everyone's favorite arc(s) are in this story? Speaking of, I've been wanting to create emotes of Rika (and now Nun-Sol) since the start, but haven't gotten around to doing so. Now that the novel has ended... Maaaaaaybe I'll get cracking on it? (heh) Anyway, Big Life is my first slice-of-life novel, and I've enjoyed translating it a lot! I've been asked if I want to translate a second novel... Does anyone have any recommendations that is... not of the dungeon/wuxia/wurim genre we commonly have here in Wuxiaworld? :D


From Veela: 

So it's finally the end. It was a smooth sailing journey with a kind message in the end. Always be kind, people!
